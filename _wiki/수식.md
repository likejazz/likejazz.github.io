# BM25
$$
\operatorname{score}(D, Q)=\sum_{i=1}^{n} \operatorname{IDF}\left(q_{i}\right) \cdot \frac{f\left(q_{i}, D\right) \cdot\left(k_{1}+1\right)}{f\left(q_{i}, D\right)+k_{1} \cdot\left(1-b+b \cdot \frac{|D|}{\operatorname{avgdl}}\right)}
$$
